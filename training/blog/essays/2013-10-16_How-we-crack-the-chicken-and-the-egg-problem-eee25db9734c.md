<h1 class="p-name">How we crack the chicken and the egg problem</h1>

<section data-field="subtitle" class="p-summary">
Hatching a Design Marketplace from Scratch at Visually
</section>
<section data-field="body" class="e-content">
<section class="section section--body section--first section--last"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--fullWidth"><figure class="graf graf--figure graf--layoutFillWidthCroppedCover graf--leading"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*M1n2J7J38BKBc4jk.jpeg" data-width="1400" data-height="588" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/fit/t/NaN/NaN/0*M1n2J7J38BKBc4jk.jpeg"><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo credit: Mark Turnauckas</figcaption></figure></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h2 name="title" id="title" class="graf graf--h2 graf-after--figure graf--title">How we crack the chicken and the egg problem</h2><h4 name="subtitle" id="subtitle" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h2 graf--subtitle">Hatching a Design Marketplace from Scratch at Visually</h4><blockquote name="764f" id="764f" class="graf graf--blockquote graf--startsWithDoubleQuote graf-after--h4">“…f<em class="markup--em markup--blockquote-em">or whatever reason, people do not get distribution. They tend to overlook it. It is the single topic whose importance people understand least. Even if you have an incredibly fantastic product, you still have to get it out to people. The engineering bias blinds people to this simple fact. The conventional thinking is that great products sell themselves; if you have great product, it will inevitably reach consumers. But nothing is further from the truth</em>.”—<a href="http://blakemasters.tumblr.com/post/22405055017/peter-thiels-cs183-startup-class-9-notes-essa" data-href="http://blakemasters.tumblr.com/post/22405055017/peter-thiels-cs183-startup-class-9-notes-essa" class="markup--anchor markup--blockquote-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank"> Peter Thiel</a></blockquote><p name="4b9c" id="4b9c" class="graf graf--p graf-after--blockquote">Marketplaces, like other network-effects laden businesses like social networks, classically suffer from the so-called ‘<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chicken_or_the_egg" data-href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chicken_or_the_egg" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">Chicken or the egg</em></a>’ problem.</p><p name="b51e" id="b51e" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">At Visually, we are constantly striving to be the <a href="https://marketplace.visual.ly/?source=medium" data-href="https://marketplace.visual.ly/?source=medium" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">world’s best marketplace for premium visual content</a>, where our design community creates infographics, motion graphics, interactive visualizations, presentations and now other forms of visual content.</p><p name="4012" id="4012" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">What follows are some key lessons that we learned in the process of starting a design marketplace from scratch and building it up to a thriving marketplace today with <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">thousands of participating designers</em></strong>, <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">hundreds of paying customers</em></strong> and <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">millions of dollars</em> in transactions</strong> in less than a year. Your milage may vary.</p><h3 name="01cb" id="01cb" class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--p"><strong class="markup--strong markup--h3-strong">Offer added value to bootstrap supply</strong></h3><p name="20d5" id="20d5" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3">Talk to your community to understand what they are looking for.</p><figure name="5577" id="5577" class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*H9IlKWAPd9jIN_B6.jpeg" data-width="640" data-height="480" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*H9IlKWAPd9jIN_B6.jpeg"><figcaption class="imageCaption"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/61063852@N00/5088741119/sizes/z/in/photolist-8KF8rR-daq3oM-9yY4Cu-9yV3zR-9yV3jv-9ngTDh-9Apnoj-9Apno1-daqpMo-awCDQY-dK8f8e-d2uXHA-ayJPbT-ayMvrN-ayJQje-ayJQWB-agsE1B-ayMvNG-ayJPMz-8RFfM1-dirQnz-8RBHhR-8RFgcE-7XfJsm-8REZid-8RBSkV-9dPvCN-8RBRfZ-auqeH1-9swmFe-9sLMni-gtnoSx-gtn9Rj-gtmEZB-gtmFt2-gtnnae-gtmGzR-gtna9J-gtn9Co-gtmesU-gtnnG6/" data-href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/61063852@N00/5088741119/sizes/z/in/photolist-8KF8rR-daq3oM-9yY4Cu-9yV3zR-9yV3jv-9ngTDh-9Apnoj-9Apno1-daqpMo-awCDQY-dK8f8e-d2uXHA-ayJPbT-ayMvrN-ayJQje-ayJQWB-agsE1B-ayMvNG-ayJPMz-8RFfM1-dirQnz-8RBHhR-8RFgcE-7XfJsm-8REZid-8RBSkV-9dPvCN-8RBRfZ-auqeH1-9swmFe-9sLMni-gtnoSx-gtn9Rj-gtmEZB-gtmFt2-gtnnae-gtmGzR-gtna9J-gtn9Co-gtmesU-gtnnG6/" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo Credi</a>t</figcaption></figure><p name="3dbd" id="3dbd" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">Four main factors enabled us to quickly grow the supply side of the marketplace:</p><p name="dacd" id="dacd" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p"><strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">Community — </strong>before we even had a marketplace, we were focused on <a href="http://thenextweb.com/apps/2012/07/18/visual-ly-launches-redesign-with-social-features-for-its-growing-community-of-nearly-145k-users/" data-href="http://thenextweb.com/apps/2012/07/18/visual-ly-launches-redesign-with-social-features-for-its-growing-community-of-nearly-145k-users/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">building out the community of designers</a>, one by one, who would later become the creative talent that would find work through our marketplace.</p><p name="a8d3" id="a8d3" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p"><strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">Portfolio — </strong>we started the site by enabling designers to upload to their Visually profiles all their infographic and data visualization projects, creating easily accessible online portfolios of their work.</p><p name="8784" id="8784" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p"><strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">Tools — </strong><a href="http://readwrite.com/2011/07/12/infographics_anticipation_builds_for_new_visualiza#awesm=~okiWycKjT4YAvl" data-href="http://readwrite.com/2011/07/12/infographics_anticipation_builds_for_new_visualiza#awesm=~okiWycKjT4YAvl" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">when we first launched our initial product</a> back in 2011, we were focused on building tools aimed at designers. This built up excitement and interest around what we were offering.</p><p name="dfcb" id="dfcb" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p"><strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">Distribution — </strong>in the early days, before we could offer much in the way of paying projects to designers, we could offer them eyeballs in the form of views, as well as recognition from posting their work to our site.</p><h3 name="6cdb" id="6cdb" class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--p"><strong class="markup--strong markup--h3-strong">Fake it till you make it</strong></h3><p name="d8f3" id="d8f3" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3">Don’t be afraid to hack one-side of the market in the early days.</p><figure name="d716" id="d716" class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*JBZULcnNLZOBx0OY.jpeg" data-width="640" data-height="480" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*JBZULcnNLZOBx0OY.jpeg"><figcaption class="imageCaption"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/candapatterson/5646412949/sizes/z/in/photolist-9AXkRp-dvnG3H-aCPikR-9FaRgG-dzcjPm-fDMj8U-e96TcP-9J3PUB-7F6zwL-8zuAXL-8Fn7uC/" data-href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/candapatterson/5646412949/sizes/z/in/photolist-9AXkRp-dvnG3H-aCPikR-9FaRgG-dzcjPm-fDMj8U-e96TcP-9J3PUB-7F6zwL-8zuAXL-8Fn7uC/" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo credit</a></figcaption></figure><p name="74d2" id="74d2" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">Initially, when we had projects coming in the door of our marketplace that we knew our supply at the time would not be able to fulfill, we would often call upon our agency partners to pick up the slack and step in to take on the project. These days, agencies that don’t have the in-house talent to fulfill a client’s project actually come to <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">us. </em>They leverage our talent pool, getting ‘on-demand’ access to our designer network and, as a result, can take on projects that they otherwise wouldn’t be able to handle in-house.</p><p name="583c" id="583c" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">In the early days of testing a new vertical, like <a href="http://create.visual.ly/" data-href="http://create.visual.ly/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">interactive infographics</a> for example, we managed new projects in-house, as a testing ground for developing a more streamlined and automated process within the marketplace. <a href="http://paulgraham.com/ds.html" data-href="http://paulgraham.com/ds.html" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Doing things that don’t scale initially</a> can really help you build up a scalable model to achieve future growth.</p><h3 name="8472" id="8472" class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--p"><strong class="markup--strong markup--h3-strong">Pick your side</strong></h3><p name="53f6" id="53f6" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">Identify which side the marketplace can absolutely not function without and then focus on that.</em></p><figure name="ddbe" id="ddbe" class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*4DbUxLw0iwtLNoXE.jpeg" data-width="640" data-height="425" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*4DbUxLw0iwtLNoXE.jpeg"><figcaption class="imageCaption"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/g_travels/7726978960/sizes/z/in/photolist-cLNMDb-bXUq8L-bXUqhW-bXUpU5-bXUq21-arpcDU-8KiTdF-8wzS8b-8TmQeF-dHok8W-9B61uF-7QLCWa-8mk7AB-7Wopdh-7Wk87B-cZJJUU-7Maipc-bAcnUN-7UGtwS/" data-href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/g_travels/7726978960/sizes/z/in/photolist-cLNMDb-bXUq8L-bXUqhW-bXUpU5-bXUq21-arpcDU-8KiTdF-8wzS8b-8TmQeF-dHok8W-9B61uF-7QLCWa-8mk7AB-7Wopdh-7Wk87B-cZJJUU-7Maipc-bAcnUN-7UGtwS/" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo credit</a></figcaption></figure><p name="337e" id="337e" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">At Visually, we started by serving a niche vertical (infographics), so it made a lot of sense to initially focus on harvesting the supply side of the market (designers), rather than focus on stoking demand.</p><p name="7fd1" id="7fd1" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Your experience may differ. Say, for example, that you are in an existing market with a high barrier to customer acquisition. It would certainly make sense to focus on bringing in the demand side to your marketplace platform first, before focusing heavily on supply.</p><p name="19f8" id="19f8" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">We did things like<a href="http://www.meetup.com/The-Visual-ly-Data-Visualization-Meetup/" data-href="http://www.meetup.com/The-Visual-ly-Data-Visualization-Meetup/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank"> created a Visually Data Visualization Meetup</a>, reached out to designers through portfolio sites like <a href="http://dribbble.com/" data-href="http://dribbble.com/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">dribbble</a> and <a href="http://www.behance.net/" data-href="http://www.behance.net/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">behance</a> and invited selected members of our community to our weekly creative brainstorming sessions.</p><figure name="1887" id="1887" class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*VfV-5jlxz90-fKVU.jpeg" data-width="600" data-height="450" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*VfV-5jlxz90-fKVU.jpeg"><figcaption class="imageCaption">Visually Creative Director, Jess Bachman presenting at one of our Visually meetups</figcaption></figure><h3 name="2d50" id="2d50" class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--figure"><strong class="markup--strong markup--h3-strong">Focus on a vertical</strong></h3><p name="c56c" id="c56c" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">Don’t Overreach: Move Toward Your Goals One Step at a Time.</em></p><figure name="5ee3" id="5ee3" class="graf graf--figure graf-after--p"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*-vVxCL2fQFmAvcmn.jpeg" data-width="640" data-height="479" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*-vVxCL2fQFmAvcmn.jpeg"><figcaption class="imageCaption"><a data-href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/editor/283989913/sizes/z/in/photolist-r6wiP-u7Znz-ujXor-v9NEg-yvXQE-yybcM-A6cyJ-AC8Bg-C6iV6-G3V1K-J2mYb-KhYRp-KP9je-LsM2W-SaRaS-TiomH-WB39v-XqGwP-2mgLN5-2oH3Aj-2GNfXm-2MveVW-2V1XZL-2WJprt-347NJ8-3o1CC2-3uNLmv-3NHWHv-3NNaDA-44hR3F-48HMBH-49D6if-4btoq9-4dFpDd-4dHyd7-4k22n3-4kaJnp-4keJXq-4keK4q-4mej4H-4ryLBG-4rXFqP-4tkbRz-4tmvM8-4wrQ33-4xySzx-4BQX6j-4C3pLm-4F6Dt1-4Lpz8L-4TFkVf/" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Photo credit</a></figcaption></figure><p name="75ca" id="75ca" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">Starting off, we decided to focus just on infographics, which were at the core of what our design community came to us for and how customers identified us. This allowed us to build out a scalable process, which now serves as a blueprint for every vertical we decide to enter.</p><p name="a878" id="a878" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">To do that, we often had to weigh the temptation to fulfill new types of demand from customers — and the need to grow the business — against our current supply and operational capacity.</p><p name="71d7" id="71d7" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Having effectively streamlined the process of infographics, we only recently ventured into offering motion graphics and interactive ones.</p><p name="0505" id="0505" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Even though our aspirations for our marketplace go far beyond what we offer today, our primary goal is to build the best platform for designers and customers to collaborate in the creation of all forms of visual content.</p><p name="c4a7" id="c4a7" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">We will reach that goal one vertical at a time.</p><p name="86ea" id="86ea" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So what are you waiting for?
